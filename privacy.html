Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/06/2025

C2XK does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. This app runs locally on your device and does not transmit any personal information to external servers. No personal information, health data, location data, or user analytics are collected. The app does send anonymous crash reports to help improve stability.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to contact us at c2xk.app@gmail.com.
